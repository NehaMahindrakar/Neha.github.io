Out with the Old


Sit, click and Explore



Designing a new Web-page is easy.
Committing your time and following through
with that original concept, that's the tricky part.


I have recently started working on Web-development.I have been learning
HTML since 2015.Whne I joined my engineering college, I stopped working on it
due to academics and other high level stuff, but I have been coming back to it
over these few months. It's undergone countless iterations.

I started working again by taking several courses on Web-development
through online platforms.I started with Cascading Style Sheets ,java-script
and bootstrap too.I would recommend it to you too.Check out online
courses, from various platforms.It did take time to learn, but it is worth it.
And also lot of fun.

If anyone is interested in learning web-design, I would love to make
documnents and papers.So that you can download them and learn easily.
In the next few weeks I'm also going to write about the decisions
I made while building out this site - inspiration, evolution,
content management, execution. Hopefully it may be of some use
to those starting out with building their first portfolio
- maybe help shave a year or two off it here and there!
